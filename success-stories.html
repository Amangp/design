Nina's Story: Pregnancy Discrimination in the Workplace

Nina experienced pregnancy discrimination when applying for a job. When she told the employer she was pregnant, they said they couldn't hire her because she would need to take maternity leave. With our help, Nina challenged this discrimination and received compensation.

Her case highlights how the Equal Opportunity Act protects against discrimination based on pregnancy and parental status in the workplace, establishing important precedent for expectant mothers seeking employment.
