Workplace Discrimination

Understanding and addressing discrimination to create equitable workplaces.

Common Types of Discrimination

Race & Ethnicity Discrimination

+

Includes racial slurs, denial of opportunities due to race, unequal enforcement of policies, and caste-based discrimination.

Gender Discrimination

+

Includes unequal pay for equal work, gender-based promotion practices, discriminatory hiring, and pregnancy discrimination.

Age Discrimination

+

Includes age-based layoffs, forced retirement, age-specific job requirements, and passing over qualified older workers.

Disability Discrimination

+

Includes failure to provide reasonable accommodations, harassment based on disability, and segregation of workers with disabilities.

How to Recognize Discrimination

Key Signs to Watch For:

  • Disparate Treatment: Similarly situated employees treated differently
  • Hostile Environment: Offensive conduct based on protected characteristics
  • Systemic Patterns: Policies that disproportionately impact protected groups
  • Retaliation: Negative consequences for reporting discrimination

Documenting Discrimination

Proper documentation is crucial when addressing workplace discrimination. Watch this guide:

5-Step Documentation Process:

  1. Keep a detailed journal of incidents
  2. Save all related communications
  3. Obtain copies of company policies
  4. Report formally following company procedures
  5. Track your performance to counter false claims

Latest Workplace News in India

Supreme Court of India Issues Landmark Judgment on Workplace Harassment +

The Supreme Court issued comprehensive guidelines expanding protections against subtle forms of discrimination and harassment in the workplace.

The ruling highlights the role of employers in creating safe and inclusive environments for all employees regardless of caste, religion, or gender.

Ministry of Labour Announces New Anti-Discrimination Rules for IT Sector +

New regulations target discriminatory practices in India's technology industry, including mandatory diversity reporting and anti-bias training.

The regulations will affect all companies with more than 100 employees and include protections for contractual workers.

April 20, 2025

Tata Group Launches Industry-Leading Anti-Discrimination Program +

The Tata Group has implemented a comprehensive program addressing caste and gender discrimination across its companies.

The initiative includes anonymous reporting systems, third-party investigations, and robust remediation processes that have already seen positive results.

April 18, 2025

Delhi High Court Rules on Religious Discrimination Case +

The Delhi High Court issued a significant ruling clarifying employer responsibilities regarding religious accommodations in the workplace.

The case centered around discrimination against a Muslim employee who was denied reasonable accommodations for prayer times.

April 15, 2025

Infosys Settles Major Caste Discrimination Case +

Leading Indian IT company agrees to ₹115 crore settlement and significant policy changes following allegations of caste-based discrimination.

The case has prompted industry-wide review of hiring and promotion practices in India's technology sector.

April 12, 2025

Remote Work Reduces Discrimination in Indian Workplaces, Study Finds +

Research by IIM Ahmedabad finds significant reductions in certain forms of discrimination in remote work environments, particularly relating to appearance, dialect, and regional background.

However, the study also notes new forms of bias emerging in virtual workplaces that companies need to address.
